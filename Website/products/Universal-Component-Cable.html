UNIVERSAL COMPONENT CABLE

  • Provides audio & video connection between your console and your TV
  • Works for PS3, Xbox 360 & Wii
  • 2.75M cable (9 feet)
  • Universal

This Component AV Cable provides audio and video connection between your console and your TV set.

The Component AV Cable is compatible with most televisions with Component AV input connectors.

It also features left and right audio plugs for direct connection to an amplifier of hi-fi system.

For connecting HDMI ready consoles to compatible High Definition televisions, the Component AV Cable is second only to the HDMI Cable.
